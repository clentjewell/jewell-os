## Why Gold beats DIY-with-AI (the quality floor)

The buyer's own AI gives them fragments. Gold ships a coherent system: strategy, brand,
site, and launch programme that agree with each other, produced through the same skills
and verification loop that built the flagship work, QA-gated by a human. The floor is the
same check-and-fix standard as everything else in this OS — if a Gold output would not
survive our own adversarial pass, it does not ship.

## Maxxim slot-in rule

The tiers are engine-agnostic by design: the promise, scope, price and gates never change
when the production engine does. When the Maxxim platform is stable, it takes over Gold
and Platinum production; Jewell stays human-led with Maxxim behind it. No tier redesign,
no re-pricing, no client-visible change — the engine swaps under the bonnet.
