# Jewell — Company Profile & Positioning

## Who we are

**Jewell** (legal/trading history: Jewell Projects; platform layer now branded
**Jewell AI**) is an Australian, founder-led business run by **Clent Jewell**
(25+ years building brands in Australia and globally; 15 years, 100+ clients,
engagements from $5K to $1.5M).

Jewell positions itself as **"an AI-powered commercialisation engine"** — not an
agency, not a consultancy, not a software platform. The authoritative positioning
line (from *Jewell — Positioning Confirmation v01*, 25 June 2026):

> "Jewell turns strong brands into commercial growth… with AI doing the volume
> and a senior operator owning the number."

- Internal category word: **commercialisation**. External, SEO-driven front-door
  word: **growth** (the front-door product is the "Growth Diagnostic").
- A **fractional CMO** framing is under consideration as the lead offer (Clent's
  call, open at time of writing) because it implies recurring revenue.
- Target buyer: founder/owner of a **$1–50M revenue** business. Secondary
  segments: (1) owners heading toward a capital event who need positioning plus
  an Information Memorandum (the Jewell Tyres / OTR Earthmover pattern), and
  (2) **Maxxim partners** — agencies and consultants who buy leverage through the
  Maxxim platform rather than a service.
- Competitive frame: agencies/consultancies sell activity without owning the
  outcome; DIY AI has no accountability; fractional CMOs and freelancers don't
  scale. Jewell's white space: "We grew Australia's biggest brands from inside
  the industry. Now we run that engine for any brand."

An earlier vision document ("Catalyst Culture — Pioneering a Purpose-Driven
Future of Work", draft v1.03) frames Jewell as a movement with an academy, hub,
and retreats. Treat it as historical/aspirational; the June 2026 positioning
document is current.

## The 3D Process™ (core methodology)

**Discover → Design → Deploy**, with a **Deepen** feedback loop. Internally it
runs as a nine-stage process (Stage 0 Onboarding through Stage 8 Blueprint
Extraction) with client checkpoints called **Gates** (Gate 1 Discovery complete;
Gate 2 Strategy and Brand approved, Clent signs; Gate 5 Final delivery, Clent
signs).

| Phase | Focus | Output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 01 Discover | Understand before building: the Why, audience/personas, competitors, business model audit, opportunity map | Discovery Summary |
| 02 Design | Strategy on the page: business plan, brand strategy, offer design, marketing/activation strategy, copy playbook and voice | Strategic Design Document |
| 03 Deploy | Translate strategy into live assets: identity, website, social, paid, email, SEO, PR, content | A business live in market |
| + Deepen | Stay embedded — measure, improve, scale; every cycle feeds the next | A compounding commercial system |

**Standard artefact codes** used across all client documents: D01–D09 (Discover),
DS01–DS05 (Design/Strategy Spine), DD0x (Deploy), IB (Intelligence Brief),
KH00 (Knowledge Hub index — the single front-door doc per client), P0x
(commercial docs: P04 Proposal, P05 SOW, P06 Contract, P07 sign-off/Welcome Pack).

## Service tiers

| Tier | Shape | Method visibility |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Good** ("Ship It") | Fixed price, brief in → deliverable out, single revision. Routes to automated intake. The majority of engagements. | Methodology deliberately **not** surfaced to the client — no stage/gate language |
| **Better** ("Aligned") | Project-based with one 60–90 min alignment session around a 1–2 page Direction Document; "two-key" sign-off (Clent + client contact), 5-business-day SLA | Methodology not shared |
| **Best** ("Customised") | Full 3D Process, stage-gated end to end, by application; discovery call entry; monthly Deepen reviews | Methodology fully shared |

Add-on products: Brand sprint, Website sprint, Content engine, SEO programme,
Paid media setup, Jewell Intelligence setup.

## Revenue model (priority order, June 2026 strategy brief)

1. **3D Process engagements** — tiered; entry via a **$4,950 Discovery Sprint**
   that credits toward a full engagement.
2. **Recurring retainers** — the "Pottsville pattern": setup/sprint plus a
   $5K+/month retainer.
3. **Maxxim / MAX platform income** — Client Partners resell the 3D Process to
   their own clients; the scale lever that doesn't consume Clent's delivery hours.
4. **Capital events** — e.g. a Jewell Tyres raise.

## Brand, voice, and values

- **One-word why: Potential** — "we see potential in people and help them
  realise it" (Brand Book 2024). Values: Connection & Empathy, Be curious, Kind,
  Integrity, Courage, Teamwork & Synergy.
- **Voice: Confident & Caring**; the feeling to leave people with is **Secure**
  ("I got you"). Client Experience deck tagline: "Marketing that's human-centred,
  AI-powered — for the disruptors, innovators and thought leaders." Mindset:
  "Strategic Partner, not Consultant — we win when you win."
- **Copy rules** (as enforced on the Jewell website): British English, short
  sentences (~12–15 words), no em dashes, no exclamation marks, numerals for
  metrics, "3D Process™" on first prominent use per page, every flow ends on
  "Next" (never "Thank you").
- **Visual identity**: colours cream, black, charcoal, calm grey, single accent
  **Signal Blue**; type **Poppins** (300/400/500/600) and **JetBrains Mono**.
  The wordmark is "Jewell" — "Projects" is retired from the brand and appears
  only in the legal entity line.
- **Principles**: Clarity before complexity · Strategy before design · Empathy
  with precision · Minimalist intelligence · Custodianship over transactions ·
  The feedback loop compounds.
