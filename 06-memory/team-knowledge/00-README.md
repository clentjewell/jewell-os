## Sources and freshness

Compiled 9 July 2026 from: the Jewell shared Google Drive (01 Clients, 02 Jewell
Group, 03 Administration, 04 Intelligence, 06 Own Projects), the Slack workspace
(jewell-projects.slack.com), and the `clentjewell` GitHub estate including the
`jewell-ai` repository. Where documents conflicted, the most recent dated document
was preferred and the conflict is noted inline.

Facts change fast (client status, pipeline, pricing). Treat anything older than a
quarter as "verify before quoting" — the canonical sources are the Drive Master
Index (04 Intelligence → 06 Master Index & Navigation) and the auto-generated
GitHub/Cloudflare registers, both refreshed nightly.

## What is deliberately excluded

This pack is shared with the whole organisation, so it excludes: everything in
`05 Personal` and the personal `#sam-*` Slack channels, personal legal/health/family
matters, individual contractor rates and salaries, client contract dollar values,
bank balances, and any document containing credentials. If a question needs that
material, it should go to Clent or Ronnie directly, not to this project.
