## Engagement lifecycle (how work is sold and run)

1. **Intake / Discovery forms** (03 Administration → 05 Forms & Inbound):
   structured questionnaires — Discovery, Audience Teardown, Competitor
   Analysis, Brand/Website Copy Workbooks, Offer Worksheet, Task Request — feed
   the Discover phase before scoping.
2. **Proposal (P04)** → **Welcome Pack** (agency intro, team, success framing)
   → **SLA** (modelled on the Hidrive/NXT template, covering the 3D phases) →
   **SOW/Contract (P05/P06)**. Standard legal requirements in every SLA:
   non-compete/exclusivity, confidentiality, IP ownership, performance
   (on-time/on-budget/on-brief). Supplier/contractor SLAs use the same brief
   with company vs sole-trader variants.
3. **Delivery** runs the 3D Process with Gate sign-offs (Gate 2 and Gate 5
   signed by Clent) and artefact codes; Asana is the system of record; weekly
   update decks for retainer clients (the Pottsville cadence).
4. **Blueprint extraction**: at maturity, reusable patterns and case-study
   material are extracted into 05 Blueprint Extraction and the Knowledge Base.

Client-specific engagement admin lives in the client's `00 Admin` folder;
cross-client commercial frameworks live in 03 Administration → 06 Engagements.
