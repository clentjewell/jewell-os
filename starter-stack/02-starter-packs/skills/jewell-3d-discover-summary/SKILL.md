# Jewell 3D Discover summary

Turn Discover-stage inputs into a complete Discover pack for the engagement.

## When to use

During the Discover stage of an engagement, once meeting notes, the client intelligence brief,
and any other Discover inputs exist for the client.

## Inputs

- Discover inputs already on file: the client intelligence brief in `00_Intelligence`, meeting
  notes, and any source material the client has shared for Discover.

## Outputs

- A Discover pack, filed in `01_Discover`: business goals, audience, competitors, problems,
  opportunities, risks, assets, constraints, and the decisions needed next.

## Steps

1. Pull every Discover input already on file for this client. Read `00_Intelligence` and any
   existing `01_Discover` material first.
2. Draft the Discover pack under the standard headings: goals, audience, competitors, problems,
   opportunities, risks, assets, constraints, decisions needed.
3. Where an input is missing or thin, flag it as a specific open question. Never fill a gap with
   an assumption dressed as a fact.
4. Check the draft does not re-ask anything the system already knows from `00_Intelligence` or
   prior notes.
5. **Approval gate.** The engagement owner reviews the pack, resolves or reassigns the open
   questions, and approves it as the current Discover pack.

## Guardrails

- Must never invent an answer to a gap. A gap is flagged as a question, not guessed.
- Must never ask what the system already knows. Check `00_Intelligence` and existing notes first.
- This is a Tier 1 internal output. Approved by the engagement owner before it is treated as the
  basis for Design work.

## Where the output is filed

That client's `01_Discover` folder.

## Example

Acme Co has a client intelligence brief and two Discover meeting notes on file. The pack drafts
goals, audience and competitors from those sources, and flags "risk appetite for the new offer
not yet discussed" as an open question rather than assuming a cautious or aggressive stance.

## Success measure

The Discover pack is complete against the standard headings, with every real gap flagged and no
guessed answers.
