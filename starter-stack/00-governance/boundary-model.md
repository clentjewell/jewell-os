# Boundary model

The two boundaries every pack in this kit must respect. Both are canonical in
`../../OPERATING-SYSTEM.md`: the three-layer Jewell × Maxxim model in Section 2, and the
work/personal boundary in Section 13 (operational quick references:
`../../00-governance/jewell-maxxim-relationship.md` and
`../../00-governance/work-personal-boundary.md`). This file only names the lines that must hold.

## Jewell × Maxxim: the lines that must hold

1. Jewell is human-led and the primary client-facing brand; Maxxim stays the engine, never the front door.
2. Maxxim must not overpower the Jewell client experience.
3. Client data must never leak into a reusable Maxxim asset; a Jewell pattern becomes Maxxim IP only after review and sanitisation.

## Work/personal: the lines that must hold

1. Personal, legal, family and sensitive matters live in a separate private space — never indexed, never surfaced to the team, never in the Ask source set. Its audience is Clent, Ronnie, and Liz as Ronnie's back-up (decided 10 July 2026) — no one else.
2. The work OS may record the pattern of a personal routine, never its content; the Today door may show a single personal reminder line.
3. If personal or legal content is found in shared Drive, Slack or a prompt, flag it to Clent; do not move it yourself.
