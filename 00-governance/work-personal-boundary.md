# Work and personal boundary

The full rules live in
[OPERATING-SYSTEM.md, Section 13](../OPERATING-SYSTEM.md#section-13-work-and-personal-boundary).

## The line

Work lives in the shared Jewell operating system. Personal, legal, family and sensitive matters
live in a separate private space. The work OS can record the pattern for a personal routine
without holding or exposing its content.

## The private space is

- Never indexed by the machine.
- Never surfaced to the team.
- Never in the Ask Jewell AI source set.
- Clent-only.

## What the Today door may show

A single personal reminder line. The content behind it stays private.

## Check now

Confirm nothing personal, legal or family sits in shared Drive, Slack or any prompt today. If it
does, flag it to Clent. Do not move it yourself.
