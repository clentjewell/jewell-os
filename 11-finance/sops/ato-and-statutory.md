# SOP — ATO, BAS and statutory payments

Quarterly BAS and PAYG, ATO payments via NAB BPAY, and the annual ASIC
fee. Covers both entities: **Jewell Group Pty Ltd** and **Jewell
Investment Group**.

**Boundary.** BAS is prepared and reviewed internally, then **Damian
(TWB Chartered Accountants)** reviews and lodges. The AI prepares the BAS
pack (from Q1 FY27 onward); it does not lodge. Payments are made by Liz
via NAB BPAY. See `QUARTERLY-BAS.md` for the cadence-level runbook — this
file is the click-level detail.

**Portal.** Damian uploads ATO documents to the TWB portal:
<https://portal.hownowhq.com>. Login is `clent@jewellprojects.com`;
**the password is in Dashlane / the Drive `Logins` doc — not in this
repo** (see [`credentials.md`](./credentials.md)).

## Entity 1 — Jewell Group Pty Ltd (Jewell Projects)

Lodges a **Business Activity Statement (BAS) every quarter**. The
accounts team completes bookkeeping and coding, does an internal review,
then sends to Damian, who reviews and lodges with the ATO and emails the
payment slip back. Because the entity is under a tax agent, BAS follows
**tax-agent lodgment due dates**:

| Quarter | Period | Due date |
|---|---|---|
| Q1 | Jul–Sep | 25 November |
| Q2 | Oct–Dec | 28 February |
| Q3 | Jan–Mar | 26 May |
| Q4 | Apr–Jun | 25 August |

### Procedure
1. **Complete bookkeeping.** All bank accounts reconciled; all
   transactions coded; GST coding reviewed; outstanding queries resolved.
2. **Internal review.** Check GST on income, GST on expenses, BAS-related
   accounts, reconciliations, and any unusual or high-value
   transactions.
3. **Send to Damian.** Notify him the quarter is ready. He reviews,
   prepares and lodges the BAS, and emails the accounts team the attached
   payment slip.
4. **Pay via NAB BPAY** — see "Processing ATO payments" below.

## Processing ATO payments (NAB BPAY)

Most payment documents are titled **ATO Payment Slip**.

1. Open the document and click **Preview**. Locate the **Biller Code**,
   the **Customer Reference Number** (payment reference), and the
   **amount payable**.
2. Log in to **NAB Internet Banking → BPAY Payments**.
3. Enter the **Biller Code**, **Reference Number** and **amount**.
4. Review carefully, then **submit**. Save the confirmation if required.

**BPAY discipline:** the biller code and reference are critical — double
check both. Set the payment **2 days before** the due date, then check
the NAB payment list to confirm it is in the future/scheduled payments.

## ASIC annual fee (NAB BPAY)

- Biller code and reference matter — **double check**.
- Set the payment **2 days before** the due date.
- Check the payment list to confirm it was added to future/scheduled
  payments.
