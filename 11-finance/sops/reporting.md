# SOP — Reporting (weekly cash flow, monthly P&L, statements)

The reporting cadence: the Monday cash-flow email, the monthly P&L and
business snapshot, the costing/PL cashflow update, and downloading the
monthly bank statement. These feed the `WEEKLY-UPDATE.md` and
`MONTHLY-CLOSE.md` runbooks.

---

## A. Weekly cash-flow reporting (Monday)

1. Open **JP Cashflow** (the shared Google Sheet) and update the
   **yellow-highlighted** cells.
2. Put the **statement closing balance** into JP Cashflow at **B2 –
   Credit Card**.
3. **Invoices** — from Accounts Receivable, pull **Awaiting Approval**
   invoices.
4. **GST / ATO** — Xero → **Accounting → Activity Statement → Prepare**
   to read the GST figure.
5. Always update **Weekly Bills**.
6. **Email Clent.** After the cashflow, chase Clent to approve the
   Awaiting Approval invoices. Also send the **P&L for Jewell** for the
   week (see the P&L procedure/link).
7. Send the **Accounts Receivable summary** email. Copy the template and
   change the date and totals, e.g.:

   > Subject: Accounts Receivables as at (today's date)
   >
   > Hi Clent, here's the summary of your Accounts Receivable as of
   > today:
   > • Total Receivables: $X
   > • Total Credit (Circl): $Y – to be applied to future invoices as
   >   agreed.
   > Let me know if you need any further breakdown.

   Insert the supporting screenshot.

**Costing & PL note.** Same flow: update the cashflow, put the statement
closing balance to **B2 – Credit Card**, and for a lower ATO payable read
it via **Accounting → Activity Statement → Prepare**. Then email Clent —
change the date up to the subject and the total, and keep the sheet link
updated.

---

## B. Monthly reporting (P&L and business snapshot)

1. **Accounting → Reports → Published → Management Report.**
2. Click **Balance Sheet** in the contents and read **Total Equity**.
   Refer to the previous draft email messages for context and wording.
3. For the **monthly business snapshot**, follow the same process as the
   daily snapshot — adjust the dates to cover the required month, then
   snip the image for the report.

---

## C. Downloading the monthly bank statement

1. Open the **NAB** website and log in.
2. Select the relevant account.
3. Set a **custom date range for the previous month** (e.g. 1 Sep 2025 to
   30 Sep 2025) and click **Update Transactions**.
4. **Download the statement as PDF.**
5. Save it in the **bank statement folder** in Accounts (Drive).
