banking rails but are governed as personal/legal matters — **Tier 3,
Clent alone** (`00-governance/approval-points.md`). The AI extracts and
queues; a human decides and pays.

---

## A. Child support — Matina Jewell

Handled through the established approval-queue process (`WEEKLY-UPDATE.md`
covers the queue). The recorded rules:

1. **Amount.** Pay **50%** of the requested amount only.
2. **Timing.** **Defer 14 days** from the date the request is received.
3. **Communication (strict).** **No correspondence with Matina.** The
   only permitted contact is the **remittance confirmation**, sent to her
   Proton email *after* payment.
4. **Processing (NAB).** Log in to NAB → **Pay Anyone** → select the
   **F1 S&J Child Support** account → payee **Matina** → enter **50% of
   the approved amount** → add the reference → submit.
5. **Records.** Update the designated child-support Google Sheet and save
   the payment receipt to the designated Drive folder.
6. **Confirm internally.** Notify Clent once paid and records updated.

Because this is a legal/personal matter, it stays **Tier 3** — the AI
never promotes a claim to paid or sends anything to Matina. It prepares
the queue entry and flags it; Liz processes and Clent decides.
